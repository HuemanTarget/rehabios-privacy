Rehab iOS Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is Pompous Cat Studios' policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you through our app, Rehab iOS.

Rehab iOS does not collect any data or information the user inputs into the app. All data is stored locally on the user's phone with no access by Pompous Cat Studios or outside networks.

The Rehab iOS user has complete control over any data they input into the app and can delete the data at anytime in app.

If you have any questions about how we handle user data and personal information, feel free to contact us.
